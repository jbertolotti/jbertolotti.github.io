Outreach on social media

We produce explicative visualizations/animations about Physical and Mathematical topics, which we share on social media, together with a brief explanation. You can find most of them by checking the hashtag #PhysicsFactlet on either Twitter or Mastodon. As a rule, we release the into the Public Domain (so you can reuse them without fear), and upload them to Wikimedia Commons.

We have now done several hundreds of them, and we will (slowly) upload them here (WIP).
