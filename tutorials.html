The following tutorials are meant to give an introduction to some advanced subjects (i.e. subjects that usually not part of a standard physics curriculum). The idea is to offer a taste of something a little bit less known to fellow physicists in other fields, student, high-school teachers or simply to people keen on science. I tried to keep the explanations as simple as possible but some knowledge about math and physics is necessary to follow these tutorials.

I decided to use the Computable Document Format (CDF) from Wolfram instead of the more common pdf because it allows to include interactive parts and because I am anyway proficient with Mathematica while I know nothing about Java and Javascript. Sadly this means a new player to download and install (from here). Sorry about that.
